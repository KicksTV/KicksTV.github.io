Our Kitchen

UI Personal 2019
Our kitchen is a fictional app designed to help refugees integrate with communities. The app acts as a directory to enable the public to book cooking lessons taught by refugees. Unlike most food based apps there are no stored recipes, this gives 
people a reason to come to the classes rather than just cooking them at home. The colours are based on the Olympic refugee flag to represent the identity, with a handwritten logo emphasising the goal of human interaction. The app acts as a bridge between refugees and public.
