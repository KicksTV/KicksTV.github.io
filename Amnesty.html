I am Listening

Charity Campaign

2018

Ni Yulan a housing right activity from China was tortured and thrown out of her thrown as she stood up to the Chinese authorities. In support of the Amnesty International campaign we produced a social campaign to help spread awareness and get people to sign a petition in support of housing rights. The large ear was the symbol of our campaign, getting
peoples attention in the cold and busy streets of London. Children would drag their parents to sign their name on the ear, excited to get involved. We produced flyers to hand out so people could read about the issue in their own time. We produced a film to reflect the mission and results of the
