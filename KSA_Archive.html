KSA Archive

Print, UX 2018

Whilst researching into the KSA Archive I found out that there is a huge lack of material and knowledge in the archive. In fact no one even knew it existed. I have proposed a solution, a monthly publication where students can share their work. Projects can be submitted

via a website which also acts as a digital gallery, where people can easily search for specific projects or people. This way we move forward from ideas within KSA, not repeating the same things.
