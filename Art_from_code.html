Art as Code

Print and UI Personal 2019
A publication to celebrate 100 years of Bauhaus by presenting the iconic work of Bauhaus in the form of code. Bauhaus was all about using new tools, technology and detaching themselves from the past so the project is also in support of their ideologies.
When you look at code it may seem overwhelming however it is all made up of simple instructions, just like shapes in the work of Bauhaus. This publication lets you see the connection and resemblance. All code made with P5.js.
I recently made a low fidelity prototype made from wireframes which presents the next idea of a website which allows you to easily create art from code.
