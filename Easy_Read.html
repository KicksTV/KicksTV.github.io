Easy Read

UI

2019

An interactive website which encourages people to read, allowing you to alter the design of the book in order to improve the reading experience. The options only supply design alterations which will increase the readability, for example the choice of 

 

typeface or the line spacing. I want people to feel like they have control over what they do, not just sitting there and reading but being able to interact to make it a more fun experience. They can drag a line which acts as a finger pointer, to show where they are,

 

change the colour to help them focus, add paragraphs. They can add as many or as little changes they want, challenging themselves to read the book. The project was based on the book Ulysses which is a famous book that many  people struggle to read.
