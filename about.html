Designer and recent graduate from Kingston School of Art.
Based in London.

Graphic design is so broad and exciting, no I don’t sit at a desk all day and just make logos or websites. I have a more hands on approach, getting deep and gritty into a subject matter. Finding out all there is to know about something in order to interpret the information as best as I can. Research is a big part of my practice, I get to be a scientist for a few hours or be in the minds of a child. My perspective changes constantly.
