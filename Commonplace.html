Commonplace

Print
2018
An experimental publication based on the downfalls of technology. A visual  representation of a file becoming damaged as its processed within technology. As you get further and further to the end you can no longer read but just see shapes 
and colours. The content itself contains segments from articles about the damage caused by technology.
